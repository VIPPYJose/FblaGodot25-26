# Maplewood Mysteries — Complete Game Plan

A single-document reference for the full playthrough, progression, plot, mechanics, and design of the 3-level top-down adventure.

---

## 1. Game Overview

| Aspect | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Title** | Maplewood Mysteries |
| **Genre** | Top-down, dialogue-driven adventure |
| **Tone** | Whimsical, school-appropriate, safe humor |
| **Protagonist** | Player-controlled kid (custom name) |
| **Companion** | Best friend Jamie (joins in Level 2) |
| **Combat** | None — environmental obstacles and puzzles only |
| **Scope** | 3 levels, ~10–12 NPCs, 4–5 items, 2–3 puzzles per level |

---

## 2. Plot Summary

Strange events hit the neighborhood: lost pets, weird noises, missing items. The player investigates, follows clues through the neighborhood, park, and school, and discovers that a young inventor’s gadgets have gone haywire. The player helps fix the situation through dialogue and simple puzzles, earning thanks from the town and a peaceful resolution.

---

## 3. Core Mechanics

### 3.1 Movement & Interaction
- **Movement:** Top-down (WASD or arrow keys); walk around streets, park, school, workshop.
- **Interaction:** Talk to NPCs and use objects (e.g. E key or click).
- **No combat:** Obstacles are environmental (boxes, fences, locked doors, squirrels, etc.).

### 3.2 Dialogue
- **Primary driver of progression:** Most advancement comes from talking to NPCs and following their hints.
- **Style:** Linear with 1–2 minor branching options.
- **Uses:** Quests, hints, tone, humor, and school-friendly stakes.

### 3.3 Inventory & Items
- **Notebook** — Collect clues; required for progression and gate triggers.
- **Flashlight** — Optional; used for environment/puzzle interaction.
- **Key** — Unlocks the workshop door (Level 3).
- **Fetch items** — Optional side quests (e.g. toy, ball).

Approximate total: **4–5 items** (clues, keys, flashlight, notebook).

### 3.4 Puzzles & Obstacles
- **Push/pull:** Move boxes to reach higher ground or clear paths (e.g. fence, pond).
- **Keys & doors:** Find key (via NPC hints) to unlock gates/doors.
- **Sequence:** Button order or simple logic (e.g. workshop machine).
- **Environment:** Squirrels blocking paths (avoid or lure); blocked paths; ladders.
- **Fetch:** Retrieve floating or stuck items (e.g. notebook in pond).

**Puzzle count:** About **2–3 per level**.

### 3.5 Progression Rules
- **Level gating:** Player cannot leave a level until key objectives are done.
- **Flow:** Linear story with clear objectives per level.
- **Companion:** Jamie (Level 2) provides hints during dialogue and puzzles.

## 4. Complete Playthrough (Step-by-Step)

### LEVEL 1 — The Neighborhood (Tutorial / Introduction)

**Spawn:** Outside the player’s home on Oakvale Street.

**Objective:** Investigate strange happenings reported by neighbors.

| Step | Action | Outcome |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | **Spawn** | Tutorial hint: *“Mom says some strange things have been happening around Oakvale… maybe I should check it out.”* |
| 2 | **Movement & interaction** | Learn to move and interact with NPCs (E key / click). |
| 3 | **Talk to Mrs. Willow** | She lost her cat, Whiskers, near the fence. First quest: search the fenced yard. |
| 4 | **Fenced yard / box** | Push a box to climb over the fence (first puzzle). |
| 5 | **Find the cat** | Locate Whiskers in the fenced area. |
| 6 | **Return to Mrs. Willow** | She thanks the player and gives the **Notebook** (track clues). |
| 7 | **Talk to Mr. Pines (shopkeeper)** | *“I’ve heard weird noises at night near the park… be careful!”* — Hints at Level 2. |
| 8 | **Optional** | Small fetch: pick up a dropped ball. |
| 9 | **Exit to Level 2** | Gate opens after collecting the notebook. |

**Level 1 focus:** Movement, talking to NPCs, first clue item, first puzzle (box + fence), first fetch (cat).

---

### LEVEL 2 — Maplewood Park / School Yard

**Spawn:** Park entrance near the town square.

**Objective:** Investigate reports, gather clues, meet companion.

| Step | Action | Outcome |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | **Talk to Kids in Park** | *“Some toys have gone missing… maybe it’s the same weird thing?”* — Introduces multiple clues. |
| 2 | **Talk to Ms. Harper (teacher)** | *“I’ve noticed strange noises in the school yard. Perhaps the culprit left a clue there.”* — Unlocks school yard. |
| 3 | **Meet Jamie** | *“I saw you investigating! Mind if I help?”* — Companion joins; unlocks hint system. |
| 4 | **Park puzzle** | Block-move to clear path (fence/pond area). |
| 5 | **Optional fetch** | Retrieve floating notebook from pond (item interaction). |
| 6 | **Talk to Playground Students** | *“I think someone’s been sneaking around at night… maybe check the workshop near the back of school.”* — Last clue before Level 3. |
| 7 | **Obtain Key** | Key from school/teacher (e.g. Ms. Harper) for workshop door. |
| 8 | **Exit to Level 3** | Workshop gate unlocks after talking to key NPCs and collecting the notebook (and key). |

**Level 2 focus:** Dialogue-heavy quests, companion hints, environmental puzzles, key item for Level 3.

---

### LEVEL 3 — Inventor’s Workshop (Confrontation / Resolution)

**Spawn:** Outside the workshop; backyard with scattered gadgets.

**Objective:** Solve the mystery and restore normalcy.

| Step | Action | Outcome |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | **Spawn** | *“This must be the workshop the students mentioned… everything looks out of place.”* |
| 2 | **Workshop door** | Use **Key** from Level 2. If no key: *“Hmm… the door won’t open… maybe there’s a key lying around.”* |
| 3 | **Enter workshop** | Meet **Alex (inventor kid)**. |
| 4 | **Dialogue with Alex** | *“Oh! You found me… I didn’t mean to cause trouble! My gadgets went haywire!”* — Reveals “culprit.” |
| 5 | **Final puzzle** | Disable malfunctioning machine: **sequence puzzle** (push 3–4 buttons in correct order). |
| 6 | **Resolution** | Alex: *“Thanks! Everything’s back to normal.”* |
| 7 | **Town reaction** | NPCs outside: *“You’re a real hero today!”* — Reward (item, badge, or recognition). |
| 8 | **Optional wrap-up** | Explore workshop, leftover items, talk to NPCs. |
| 9 | **Companion recap** | Jamie: *“That was quite the adventure… want to explore again tomorrow?”* |

**Level 3 focus:** Story resolution, single key puzzle (door), sequence puzzle (machine), peaceful resolution, optional epilogue.

## 5. Game Progression (Flow)

```
Level 1 (Neighborhood)
	│
	├── Spawn → Tutorial hint
	├── Mrs. Willow → Cat quest → Box puzzle → Notebook
	├── Mr. Pines → Park hint
	└── Gate opens (Notebook) ──────────────────────────┐
														│
Level 2 (Park / School)                                 │
	│                                                   │
	├── Spawn at park entrance                          │
	├── Kids → Missing toys clue                        │
	├── Ms. Harper → School yard + Key                  │
	├── Jamie joins → Hint system                       │
	├── Park puzzle (block-move)                        │
	├── Playground Students → Workshop hint             │
	└── Gate opens (key NPCs + notebook) ──────────────┤
														│
Level 3 (Workshop)                                      │
	│                                                   │
	├── Spawn outside workshop                          │
	├── Key → Open door                                 │
	├── Alex → Story reveal                             │
	├── Sequence puzzle → Machine off                  │
	└── Resolution → Reward → Optional wrap-up          │
														│
	└──────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
		 (Linear: cannot skip levels or key objectives)
```

**Gating summary:**
- **Level 1 → 2:** Have **Notebook** (from Mrs. Willow).
- **Level 2 → 3:** Talk to key NPCs, have **Notebook**, obtain **Key** (e.g. from Ms. Harper).
