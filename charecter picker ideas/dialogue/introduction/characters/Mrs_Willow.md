# Mrs. Willow

**Character Name:** Mrs. Willow

**Role:** Neighbor — the first NPC the player talks to; gives the cat fetch quest and rewards the player with the Notebook.

**Personality:** Warm, fussy, and grateful. She worries about small things but means well. Prone to over-thanking people and giving life advice. She notices more about the neighborhood than she lets on, and her offhand comments often hint at something bigger going on.

**Skin Color:** Light olive.

**Hairstyle:** Silver-white hair pulled into a soft bun with a few wispy strands escaping near her temples.

**Eye Color:** Pale blue-grey, kind and slightly watery.

**Outfit:** A floral apron over a lilac cardigan and a long cream skirt. Comfortable house slippers she forgot to change before coming outside.

**Accessory:** A small pair of reading glasses perched on the end of her nose (she rarely uses them but never takes them off).

**Idle Animation / Body Language:** Clasps her hands together anxiously, occasionally glancing toward the fence. Rocks gently on her heels when listening. Reaches to adjust her bun when flustered.

**Voice Tone:** Soft, warm, and slightly breathless — like someone who's been worrying all morning. Speaks with gentle urgency. Occasionally drifts into reminiscing mid-sentence before catching herself.
