# MC

**Character Name:** MC (player-named)

**Role:** Protagonist — the player-controlled kid investigating Oakvale's strange happenings.

**Personality:** Curious, friendly, and slightly nervous. Quick to help neighbors and always ready to poke around somewhere they probably shouldn't. Has a knack for noticing details others overlook.

**Skin Color:** Customizable (left to player imagination / future character select).

**Hairstyle:** Short, slightly messy — the kind that happens when you've been running around all afternoon.

**Eye Color:** Brown.

**Outfit:** Casual everyday wear: a light hoodie, jeans, and worn sneakers. A small backpack hangs on one shoulder (holds clues and notebook).

**Accessory:** Backpack with a keychain shaped like a magnifying glass.

**Idle Animation / Body Language:** Shifts weight from foot to foot, occasionally glancing around curiously. Stands with hands tucked into hoodie pocket when not interacting.

**Voice Tone:** Warm and slightly hesitant — speaks like a kid who's genuinely trying to be helpful but is still figuring things out. Internal monologue lines are more confident and observational.
