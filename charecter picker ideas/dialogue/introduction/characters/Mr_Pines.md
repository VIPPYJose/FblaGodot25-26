# Mr. Pines

**Character Name:** Mr. Pines

**Role:** Shopkeeper — the second NPC the player must talk to in Level 1; provides the park hint and foreshadows the inventor subplot (Alex).

**Personality:** Gruff but genuinely helpful. A no-nonsense observer who's seen enough of the neighborhood to trust his gut. Slightly sarcastic, but never unkind. Underneath the dry exterior, he's clearly worried — and glad someone is finally paying attention.

**Skin Color:** Dark brown.

**Hairstyle:** Short salt-and-pepper hair, cropped close to the head. Well-kept but unpretentious.

**Eye Color:** Dark brown, steady and direct.

**Outfit:** A plain forest-green shopkeeper's apron over a white button-up shirt with the sleeves rolled to the elbows. Sturdy work trousers and boots that have seen better days.

**Accessory:** A pencil tucked behind one ear (habit from years of marking inventory). A small "Open" sign on the counter beside him, flipped to "Sort of open" when he's distracted.

**Idle Animation / Body Language:** Leans on the counter with arms folded, watching the door. Taps the pencil on the counter when thinking. Stands up straight when something gets his attention.

**Voice Tone:** Low, measured, and deliberate — speaks in short sentences and lets silences do the work. Occasionally lets dry humor slip through with a raised eyebrow rather than a laugh.
